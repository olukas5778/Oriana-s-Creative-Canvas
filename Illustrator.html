Graphic Design

I designed these logos using Illustrator for the restaurant "Lolita's Kitchen" through my internship. The owner wanted an elegant logo utilizing black, gold, and marble. For the first design on the left, I used gold and chose a font that resembled a marble design and connected the fork and knife to the letters to demonstrate the idea of a homestyle kitchen. The second logo, which is the one the owner chose, used a floral wreath to show delicacy and the L and K centered to show simplicity. The cutting board on the right was more of a random creation I did as cutting boards are a universal sign for cooking.

I designed these posters for a veterans day post for my internship Runningname LLC, The Whistleblowers of America, and the National Workplace Bullying Coalition. For the first design I wanted to feature the significance of a father and daughter relationship and the importance of acknowledging our soldiers. I used Illustrator to create the silhouette of the soldier and the various shapes incorporated in the design. I also chose to use the "Bootcamp" font to resemble military writing. For the two other designs, my supervisor wanted to incorporate a "cyber splash" into the designs so I did that by including digitized images and drawings.

This is branding that I worked on for a new project for my internship. The brand named "Dope Digital '' is a program that provides pro-bono technical services and guidance to small businesses and non-profits organizations. For this logo, my supervisor wanted it to highlight the theme of "digital transformation". I created these three variations using illustrator. I then created several mockups utilizing the design and they ended up using the center logo.
